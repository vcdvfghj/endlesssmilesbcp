Our Mission

The Endless Smiles Foundation at Basis Cedar Park serves a fundamental role in the community of the underprivileged. We offer financial, social, and emotional support for victims of chronic life-threatening diseases. By working with high profile organizations such as St. Jude's Children Research Hospital, we receive access to the diverse worlds of hospitalized kids, allowing us to keep patients happy and healthy.


Your Impact


The Endless Smiles Foundation has a profound impact on the lives of underprivileged children throughout the country. By joining, you would be a fundamental part of the recovery of

life-threathened patients, changing the world for the better. By donating, you allow us to provide the necessary support and services that keep these children happy and healthy.
